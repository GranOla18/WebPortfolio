Description

Since I was little I can remember I really liked drawing, but it wasn’t until I was in secondary school that I starting drawing in digital in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. I have been testing several bitmap apps for drawing like MediBang and Krita, though I’m more used to work in MediBang. I still feel I haven’t stablished a style yet. I try to share my work on my Instagram @xin_the_mushroom, but haven’t had time in the last months.

About

I am a fifth semester student in Animation and Video Game Engineering at Universidad Panamericana in Mexico City. I love drawing (traditional and digital), programming and learning new things. I am always looking to improve my artistic and programming skills and to achieve a balance between both of them as to learn new painting skills and learn new programming languages.
