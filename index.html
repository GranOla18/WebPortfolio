I am always looking to improve my artistic and programming skills and to achieve a balance between both of them as to learn new painting skills and learn new programming languages.

Academic Background

High School

I went to high school at Universidad Motolinía del Valle. Last year I was in Area I: Physical-Mathematical Sciences and Engineering, as I knew I wanted to study some Engineering. I ended up being the best average of my generation.

Universidad

I am currently studying the fifth semester of my degree in Animation Engineering and Video Games at the Universidad Panamericana.

Work Experience

During my third semester of college I had the opportunity to work as a Pixframe intern for a project for UNICEF, MatematIA, in which I had to do assets for some of the mathematics educational videos and various scenarios from around the world. I had to do everything on Adobe Illustrator.

I currently work as an intern at the Computer Center of the Universidad Panamericana in Mexico City. I am developing a Chatbot in Dialogflow to assist students instead of having to contact the Computer Center. I am also part of the Technical Development team at the UP’s PCI Lab, in where I have developed the player’s camera, helped making the navmesh for the enemies, which is developed with an AI integrated in Unity, and one of the main character’s powers, the Time/Sad power, which slows the time around the player, allowing him to move normally on a slowed world, for a period of time. If this power is overused there will be a punishment for the player, which is that now he will be slowed, but the surrounding time will be normal.
